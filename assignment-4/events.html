Competitions & Activities

Below is a list of competitions organized during TechFest 2025.

  1. CodeSprint — A 3-hour competitive programming contest (individual).
  2. Robotics Challenge — Team event (max 4 members) focusing on line-following and obstacle tasks.
  3. Paper Presentation — Present your research or project (individual or team).
  4. Hackathon (24h) — Team event for building a working prototype in 24 hours.
  5. UI/UX Design — Design a single-page app prototype and present the flow (individual/team).
  6. Quiz Bowl — Inter-departmental quiz (teams of 3).

Notes

  • Team size limits are specified per event.
  • Registration required for all participants via the Register page.
  • For rules and judging criteria, check the event-specific guidelines provided at the venue.
